Lunch with Tom Monaghan
0 Comments

Yesterday I was lucky enough to have lunch at White Hut in West Springfield with Tom Monaghan, the founder of Domino’s Pizza. He also founded Ave Maria University and has been a past owner of the Detroit Tigers baseball team. We had a great conversation discussing burgers while he had a “Special with fried onions”. He is opening a gourmet burger restaurant in Florida very soon and wanted to try a good burger while he was in town on business.
He is a great philanthropist and business man and we wish him well.
